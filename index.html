Essence, 2023
set of 4, digital illustration. 4665 x 4665 px each.
‘Essence’ is based on the four symbols of Chinese direction: Black Tortoise of the North, Vermillion Bird of the South, Azure Dragon of the East, and White Tiger of the West. Flowers grow from the bones of origins, representing the new diaspora growing from traditional patterns--a combination of the now and then, the here and there.
