Deliberate, 2023
set of 4, digital prints on vellum.
5' w x 8' h x 6" d
Deliberate is a set of 4 digital prints secured in float frames and suspended from an overhead beam in a wave-like formation.

front of installation.
side view.
installation reverse side.

This set of illustrations is based on Li Bai's "Drinking Alone under the Moon," an exploration of existence through metaphors of drunkenness. Beginning alone under a flowering tree at full moon, Li Bai ascends from reality to the fantastical–contemplating ideas of ambitions and life intertwined with luck and nature. In the end, he sinks back down to reality–sitting alone under the moon once again.
