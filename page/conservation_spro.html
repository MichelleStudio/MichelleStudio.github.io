Ara Pacis South Procession

RESTORATION 2024

The Ara Pacis has many decorated panels celebrating a new age of peace and prosperity. Of note is the South Procession, featuring historical figures, priests, women, and children. Following the efforts of a 6-person team, I improved the overall continuity and homogeneity of the restoration. I refined skin tones, applied facial details (eyelashes, iris, lips, and blush), reconstructed hands, and fully illustrated the 15 missing lictors.
