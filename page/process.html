Upper Kensington Commercial Circle

Photography Documentary

Though I walk through this intersection practically every day, I visited this intersection only twice with my camera: once in the morning before the fog had time to burn off, and another time once it had gone dark. I began by mostly following 1950s historical photos I found in Kensington: Past and Present (Centennial Edition). That is, I tried matching the perspectives once photographed. Because I was mostly comparing before and after, I found editing to black-and-white to be more compatible. I aimed to describe the intersection as much as I could, documenting the buildings and streets connecting to it.
