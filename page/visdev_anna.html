Anna in the Tropics

DESIGN 2024

Though Anna in the Tropics is about a cigar factory dealing with industrialization and double standards, it is more so a play about the relationships we negotiate as families—as husbands and wives, children and siblings. Inspired by the striking shapes of Matisse’s paper cutouts and the vibrant colors of vintage Cuba travel posters, this design follows the plot by relationships and presents what it truly means to be "tropical."
