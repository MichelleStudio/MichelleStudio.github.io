Between Worlds, 2023
digital photograph. 3047 x 5630 px.
Overlapping shadows of a Buddha and his four guardians exhibit the malleability of Asian identity: of Many becoming One, and of One becoming Many.
Deliberate (exhibited), 2023
set of 4, prints on vellum. 5'w x 8'h x 6"d.
This set of illustrations draws inspiration from Li Bai's "Drinking Alone under the Moon," an exploration of existence through metaphors of drunkenness. Hung in float frames in a wave-like pattern, 'Deliberate' gently sways due to undetectable currents and invites the viewer to contemplate one's own ambitions in the complex nature of society.
Deliberate (digital), 2023
set of 4, digital illustration. 2400 x 8250px each.
Beginning alone under a flowering tree at full moon, Li Bai ascends from reality to the fantastical–contemplating ideas of ambitions and life intertwined with luck and nature. In the end, he sinks back down to reality–sitting alone under the moon once again.
Occupation (full length), 2022
wool, horn toggle buttons. 54"w x 48"h x 2"d.
Occupation is a European-like coat constructed using endemic Chinese sewing techniques—proposing an alternate history where authentic form is preserved through co-opted material use. The base of this two piece set is inspired by the qipao, the traditional dress of the 1800s, a time where China—specifically Shanghai—was forcefully occupied by European countries.
Occupation (detail), 2022
wool, horn toggle buttons. 54"w x 48"h x 2"d.
‘Occupation’ blends the materiality of these "international settlements" (wool instead of silk, a coat instead of a dress, horn toggles instead of frog knots) with traditional Chinese construction (cut on flat, binding seams).
Essence, 2023
set of 4, digital illustration. 4665 x 4665 px each.
‘Essence’ is based on the four symbols of Chinese direction: Black Tortoise of the North, Vermillion Bird of the South, Azure Dragon of the East, and White Tiger of the West. Flowers grow from the bones of origins, representing the new diaspora growing from traditional patterns--a combination of the now and then, the here and there.
24 Square Blocks, 2023
web art (SVG, text, Javascript). 1440 x 810 px.
San Francisco's Chinatown remains both a racial confine and a place for cultural community. It is both an adaptation to a new society and a replica of China. In a modern interpretation of this historic space, visitors are encouraged to interact with the physical space of SF Chinatown, while uncovering the Confucius tenets that underpin its development and values. Hover over buildings to reveal what they do for the community.

Transient, 2023
Still from Video loop. 1440 x 810 px.
Snakes and cherry blossoms both are symbols of Spring, a time of renewal and the potential for change. Remixed into the digital landscape, "time" passes but nothing actually changes. In an infinite cycle of day and night, 'Transient' compares the ephemeral and preserved, and interrogates our faith in digitalization in the face of deteriorating nature.
Reduction, 2023
Still from Video loop. 1920 x 1080 px.
Pruning is paramount in real-world gardening. To be able to prune, to cut off redundancies, is to be able to "train" and invest in a living organism. However, in the digital world there is no clean removal, no real reduction. 'Reduction' questions the obsolescence of once useful tools in a space that is forever multiplying and expanding.
Hi(gh) Tide, 2023
generative art (PNGs, Javascript). 1200 x 650 px.
Interpreting the ocean not as a site of expansive contemplation, but rather of contemporary surveillance, ‘Hi(gh) Tide’ is a simulation of a full-room installation where cameras populate based on human movement--disrupting the act of seeing and being seen. Click the screen to initiate the sound.

Natural Progression, 2023
generative art (JPGs, MP3s, Javascript). 1396 x 783 px.
'Natural Progression' is a digital simulation of high-risk California native species population growth and decay. A proposal for a full-room installation, the population of digital plants rely solely on human disturbance (walking, talking, sudden noises) and ambient regeneration (rain, stability). It is an interactive contemplation of the human relation to nature and if coexistence is even possible. Click anywhere on the screen to initiate the sound.

Vigor, 2022
digital photograph. 5400 x 3600 px.
'Vigor' dismantles the human construct of Life as the opposite of Death by exhibiting them in tandem, feeding each other in an endless cycle. Proposing an alternative idea of "living," viewers are invited to consider a different perspective on the condition of existence.
Refuel, 2022
digital photograph. 3906 x 3018 px.
A tiny gas station at the edge of a tiny town, 'Refuel' centers the momentary pause in a easy-to-pass-over intersection. Both a mass delivery by an oil tanker and a small collection by a consumer, 'Refuel' considers the distribution of limited natural resources that none own, but all use.
Digitized Aidonia finds, 2023
digital documentation. 17158 x 6892 px.
Archaeology has a long history of being exploitative, of extracting value from the original country. However, by digitizing scale drawings of finds, knowledge can be distributed without removal from its origins. These specific examples that I have digitized are from Aidonia, showcasing the design sensibilities of the ancient Greece.
Ara Pacis - South Procession, 2023
digital restoration. 19616 x 3448 px.
In antiquity, almost every statue and frieze of importance was painted vibrantly, to bring those depicted truly to life. However, these days, only traces of the pigments remain. Today, the Ara Pacis--a celebration of peace--remains somberly gray. Through guided research, I restored and recolored the South Procession, bringing the parade back to its original liveliness through digital, non-destructive means.
