Concept Poster
RE:Solo, a reusable party cup that is stackable, easy to clean, and keeps drinks cool.

Difficulty-Importance Matrix
After a quick discussion, our group decided on the low difficulty-high importance quadrant for a product that has high impact with a smaller research and development investment. We soon realized that these two products could be combined, as one informs the other (i.e. keeps cool needs a lid). In this way, we decided on designing a reusable SOLO cup, one that keeps drinks cool but also fulfills the service of an easy clean-up party cup.
