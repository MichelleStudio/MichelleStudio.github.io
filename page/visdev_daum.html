Drinking Alone under the Moon

ART 2024

This set of illustrations is based on 8th century poet Li Bai’s “Drinking Alone under the Moon,” an exploration of existence through metaphors of drunkenness. Hung in float frames in a wave-like pattern, these swaying prints on vellum invites the viewer to contemplate one's own ambitions in the complex nature of society.
Exhibited in the Spring 2023 Senior Thesis Exhibition at Worth Art Ryder Gallery.
